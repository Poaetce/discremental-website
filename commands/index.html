Discremental

version a.2.2

Commands

disc - Gives player discs, multiplied by the amount of multipliers

disc help - Directs player here owo

disc check [player] - Shows the player's discs, multipliers and bots, self if not entered

disc donate [player] [amount] - Donates amount to player

disc clear - Clears all player's data

disc multi [amount]- Buys multipliers by the amount entered, 1 if non entered, enter negative number to sell

disc bot [amount]- Buys bots by the amount entered, 1 if non entered, enter negative number to sell
